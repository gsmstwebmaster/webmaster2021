Background Info

Our founder, William Yoon, graduated from GSMST’s prestigious medical program and was inspired to create this company due to a personal experience with his best friend. After his close friend Eugene, the other founder, was bedridden and unable to move due to a horrible infection, William knew he had to take action so that Eugene and others like him could get adequate medical care and attention. After Eugene’s recovery, William and Eugene founded Caducous Telecoms in 2016 to make medical health care more accessible to everyone. Currently, Caduceus Telecoms is serving patients internationally and becoming increasingly important in today’s world, especially during the Pandemic.

COVID-19



Covid-19

COVID-19 is caused by a coronavirus called SARS-CoV-2. With it comes a range of mild to severe symptoms, sometimes even asymptomatic. It is important to follow CDC Guidelines and socially distance and wear a mask in public settings. The CDC has recommended being at least 6 feet apart and has small gatherings indoors. It recommended getting tested and staying in quarantine for 14 days if in contact with someone who has covid. Common symptoms include chills, body aches, a cold, and fever. If you or a loved one need to get tested please visit our testing page to schedule a test.

We offer two types of diagnostic tests: molecular tests and antigen tests. These two tests show if an individual has an active infection. Molecular tests use PCR and Gel Electrophoresis to identify the prevalence of the COVID-19’s mRNA while antigen tests detect “spike proteins” created from COVID-19.
